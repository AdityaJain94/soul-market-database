Questions, answered

What is actually inside a kit?

Each product page lists every item. Kits hold rider-tested essentials only, no filler. The durable tools are yours to keep; the consumables are what a refill replaces.

How do refills and subscriptions work?

Choose Subscribe & Refill on any kit or bag. We ship a fresh top-up of the consumables every 3 months at a lower per-refill price. Skip a cycle or cancel anytime.

Do you ship across India?

Yes, pan-India. Orders dispatch in 2 to 4 working days. Both prepaid and cash on delivery are supported. Free shipping over ₹999.

Do the kits include medicines?

No. For legal reasons our medical kit ships dressings and first-aid supplies only, with a printed checklist of medicines to add from your pharmacy. We never sell prescription or OTC drugs.

What is your return policy?

Unopened kits can be returned within 7 days of delivery for a refund. Consumables and opened hygiene items are non-returnable for safety reasons.

Who is SOUL?

SOUL is a direct-to-consumer brand of Apex Essential Pvt Ltd (AEPL), built for India's long-distance motorcycle community.
